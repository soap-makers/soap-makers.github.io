2. Trademark Status

The Soapmakers logo and brand name are trademarks of Soapmakers, with applications pending under the South Australian Trademarks Act. Additionally, our products are protected under intellectual property laws, including copyright and trade secrecy provisions, as delineated in the South Australian Intellectual Property Act.

You can verify the trademark status of Soapmakers by visiting the Australian Trademarks Online Search System website.

3. Compliance with Laws and Regulations

Soapmakers is committed to compliance with all relevant laws and regulations governing soap making in South Australia. Our operations adhere to industry standards and legal requirements to ensure the highest quality and safety standards for our products. Key regulations include:

  • • Food Act 2001
  • • Health (Prescribed Activities) Regulations 2017
  • • National Trade Measurement Regulations 2009
  • • Work Health and Safety Act 2012
  • • Environment Protection Act 1993

We also recommend visiting the South Australia Legislation website to check the relevant laws and regulations governing soap making in South Australia.
