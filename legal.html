The Soapmakers logo and brand name are trademarks of Soapmakers, with applications pending under the South Australian Trademarks Act. Soapmakers is committed to compliance with all relevant laws and regulations governing soap making in South Australia. Our operations adhere to industry standards and legal requirements to ensure the highest quality and safety standards for our products. Key regulations include:
