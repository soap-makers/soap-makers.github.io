Care Instructions:

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep soap dry between uses to prolong its life.

Useful Information:

This coconut oil soap is suitable for all skin types and is gentle enough for daily use. It is free from harsh chemicals and artificial fragrances.

Warnings:

For External Use Only: Our soap bars are intended for external use on the skin only. Avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes. If contact occurs, rinse thoroughly with water.

Avoid Contact with Eyes: Keep the soap out of eyes. If soap comes into contact with eyes, rinse immediately with plenty of water. Seek medical advice if irritation persists.

Discontinue Use If Irritation Occurs: In case of skin irritation or rash, discontinue use immediately and consult a healthcare professional. Individuals with sensitive skin or allergies should perform a patch test before full use.

Consult a Healthcare Professional: If you have any concerns about using our soap bars, consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice and guidance.
